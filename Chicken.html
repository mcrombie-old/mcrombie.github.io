A Chicken's Tale

Initial Storyboard

1.A chicken is established as gthe protagonist 2.The chicken realizes he is lonely 3.He flys off in search of it 4.He has trouble doing so 5.He finds love 6.He hits a snag when he has to deal with her egg 7.He saves the egg despite her leaving him 8.They get back together

Appeal

I mean just look at this stud. Need I say more?

Staging

The staging of this highlights the awkwardness of the egg laying. You can see the contrasting expressions on the character's faces. The guy looking at them from behind adds to the building discomfort.

Squash and Stretch

The chicken is in love and his heart squashes and stretches to show it.

Anticipation

The egg is rolling off to its doom right after we panned away from a determined looking protagonist! What could happen next...

Secondary Action

The chicken's wings flap to keep him from suspended in the air. Kindly have enough imagination to accept chickens flying.
