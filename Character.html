Character Animation

4/26/17

Assignment

For this project, create a short animation using a pre-defined character animated through motion capture in an interesting setting. You may use dynamics, motion paths, or key-framed animation to enhance your animation. Feel free to create environmental elements, props, or even other characters to realize your goals.

Description

Initial Storyboard

A man breaks into a cottage to take out a spy drone.

Left Hand Adjusted

The left hand was orginially framed to be intersecting with the chracters face often. I adjusted it so it would not do this and so he tends to hold the gun with both hands. The part where the gun interesects with his face is also removed.

The Drone

The drone is flees the character upon entry. Note the character follows the path of the drone with his eyes and keeps his gun aimed at him.

The Cottage

The scene takes place at a small cottage.
