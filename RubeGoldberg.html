Rube Goldberg Machine Animation

Initial Storyboard

The erroneous story involves a wand guiding the eye of a hat monster back to its socket, and a ball of dough going down a slide to set off a process that cooks it into bread for the hat monster to eat. The bunny is for kicks.

Hammer

The hammer merely serves as a lever to position the eye ball for the wand to shoot it into the hat.

Wand

The wand flys, uses particle effects, and knocks the eye into the hat.

Fork

The fork is used by the hat monster to move objects around.
