Ball Animation

3/2/17

Assignment

For this project, create a short animation featuring a bouncing ball. You may tell a short story in your animation, or simply show the bouncing ball performing interesting moves; however, strive for realistic (i.e., physics-based) movement. Start with completing the bouncing ball tutorial in Chapter 8 if you like. You must employ at least three of the Disney 12 principles of animation discussed in class.

Description

Initial Storyboard

The story follows a yellow ball as it bounces through a cave. As it comes to rest it is nearly licked up by a sentient potato! The story makes use of squash and stretch, anticipation, and arcs.

Anticipation

Anticipation created through the balls emerging smile as he is floating upward only to be smashed against the ceiling...

Arc

Ball follows an arc as it bounces against the ground.

Exaggeration

The ball's fall after colliding with the ceiling shows an exaggerated stretched form of it's once chipper self plop back onto the ground.

Secondary Action

The potato's eye's follow the ball as it moves, which is not immediately obvious since the focus of attention is on the ball.

Squash and Stretch

The ball's vertical size is reduce as it impact the ground and expands to beyond normal briefly as it moves back up before returning to normal. This creates the semblence of softness.
