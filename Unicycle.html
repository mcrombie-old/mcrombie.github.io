Unicycle Animation

3/23/17

Assignment

For this project, create a short animation running 10-20 seconds that features a unicycle following a motion path. You may tell a short story in your animation, or simply show the unicycle following an interesting path; however, strive for realistic (i.e., physics-based) movement. Feel free to create environmental elements to enhance your animation, but at least include a ground plane to give the unicycle a basis in space. You must employ at least three of the Disney 12 principles of animation discussed in class.

Description

Initial Storyboard

The story follows a unicycle as it navigates around a warehouse of crates.

Anticipation

The Unicycle narrowly avoids the spikes, celebrates, and then the pause before being crsuhed by the crate builds anticipation.

Arc

The Unicycle follows an arcs after rolling off the ramp.

Secondary Action

A sign eludes to the impending crate being dropped.
